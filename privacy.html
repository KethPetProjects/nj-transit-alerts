1. Overview

NJ Transit Delay Alerts ("we," "us," or "our") operates a train delay notification service that sends SMS alerts to NJ Transit commuters. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal information when you use our service at https://black-plant-0162ad510.4.azurestaticapps.net.

2. Information We Collect

We collect only the minimum information necessary to provide the service:

  • Mobile phone number — to send you SMS train alerts
  • Train preferences — your selected morning and evening train numbers
  • Station and line — your home station and commute corridor
  • Notification preferences — whether you want delay alerts, on-time confirmations, or both

We do not collect your name, email address, payment information, or any other personal details.

3. SMS Messaging & Text Alerts

SMS Consent & Opt-In: By subscribing through our website and entering your verification code, you expressly consent to receive recurring automated SMS text messages from NJ Transit Delay Alerts at the mobile number you provided. These messages include train delay alerts, cancellation notices, and on-time confirmations for your selected trains.

Message frequency: Message frequency varies based on train delay activity. You may receive multiple messages per day during periods of high delay, or no messages on days when your trains run on time.

Message and data rates may apply. Standard SMS and data rates from your mobile carrier may apply to messages you receive.

How to opt out: You can unsubscribe at any time by:

  • Replying STOP to any SMS alert you receive
  • Using the Unsubscribe option on our website
  • Using Nova, our AI assistant, to cancel your subscription

After opting out, you will receive one final confirmation message and no further alerts will be sent.

For help: Reply HELP to any message or visit our website for support.

Supported carriers: AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and most major US carriers. We are not responsible for delayed or undelivered messages due to carrier issues.

4. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect exclusively to:

  • Send you SMS train delay and on-time alerts for your selected trains
  • Verify your phone number via a one-time code during sign-up
  • Process unsubscribe requests when you cancel your subscription

We do not use your information for marketing, advertising, or any purpose other than delivering the train alert service you subscribed to.

5. Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties except:

  • Twilio — our SMS delivery provider, used solely to transmit alerts to your phone number. Twilio's privacy policy is available at twilio.com/legal/privacy
  • NJ Transit — we access their public train schedule API to obtain delay information. No personal data is shared with NJ Transit.

6. Data Storage & Security

Your phone number and train preferences are stored in a secure PostgreSQL database hosted on Supabase with industry-standard encryption. We retain your data only as long as your subscription is active. When you unsubscribe, your data is permanently deleted from our database.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we hold about you
  • Delete your subscription and associated data at any time
  • Opt out of SMS messages at any time by replying STOP

8. Children's Privacy

This service is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated date. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
