How it works:
  1. Pick your train line, home station, and the trains you take each way.
  2. Enter your phone number — it's your account ID so you can manage or cancel later. No SMS will be sent. Alerts are delivered via the free ntfy app.
  3. We check your trains every 2 minutes. You'll get an alert if there's a delay or cancellation, and an on-time heads-up ~30 minutes before your train departs your station.

💡 Tip: Share your alert topic with your spouse or family — so they know you're running late before you even get off the train.
