How it works:
  1. Pick your train line, home station, and the trains you take each way.
  2. Choose how to receive alerts — push notifications via the free ntfy app (no phone needed), or SMS text if you prefer.
  3. We check your trains every 5 minutes. You'll get an alert if there's a delay or cancellation, and an on-time heads-up ~30 minutes before your train departs your station.
