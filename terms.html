Service Description

NJ Transit Delay Alerts is a notification service that provides SMS alerts about train delays, cancellations, and on-time confirmations for selected NJ Transit trains. The service includes:

  • Real-time delay notifications (sent immediately when delays are detected)
  • On-time confirmations (sent 30 minutes before scheduled departure)
  • Cancellation alerts
  • Ability to select morning and evening trains

Subscription and Opt-In

To use this service, you must:

  • Provide a valid mobile phone number
  • Verify your phone number via SMS verification code
  • Select at least one train to monitor
  • Explicitly consent to receive SMS notifications
SMS Consent: By subscribing, you agree to receive automated SMS text messages from NJ Transit Delay Alerts at the phone number you provided. Message frequency varies based on train status. Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.

Opt-Out / Unsubscribe

You may unsubscribe at any time by:

  • Visiting our website and using the unsubscribe feature

Upon unsubscribing, you will no longer receive notifications, and your data will be deleted within 30 days.
