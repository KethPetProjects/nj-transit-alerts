2. Description of Service

NJ Transit Delay Alerts is an independent, free SMS notification service that monitors NJ Transit train schedules and sends text message alerts to subscribers when their selected trains are delayed, cancelled, or running on time. This service is not affiliated with, endorsed by, or operated by NJ Transit.

3. SMS Terms & Consent

By subscribing, you consent to receive recurring automated SMS text messages from NJ Transit Delay Alerts at the mobile number you provided. These messages include train delay alerts, cancellation notices, and on-time confirmations.

Opt-in process: You subscribe by visiting our website, entering your phone number, selecting your trains, checking the consent checkbox, and entering a 6-digit verification code sent to your phone.

Message frequency: Varies based on train activity. You may receive multiple messages per day or none at all depending on your trains' performance.

Cost: This service is free. However, message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier plan.

To opt out: Reply STOP to any message at any time. You will receive one final confirmation and no further messages will be sent. You may also unsubscribe via our website.

For help: Reply HELP to any message or visit our website.

Supported carriers: AT&T, Verizon, T-Mobile, Sprint, and most major US carriers.

Always verify train status through official NJ Transit channels before making travel decisions. NJ Transit alerts are available at njtransit.com and the NJ Transit mobile app.
