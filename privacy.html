Privacy Policy

NJ Transit Delay Alerts

Introduction

NJ Transit Delay Alerts ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your personal information when you use our train delay notification service.

Information We Collect

We collect the following information when you subscribe to our service:

  • Phone Number: Used to send SMS notifications about train delays
  • Train Preferences: Your selected morning and evening train numbers
  • Notification Preferences: Whether you want delay alerts and/or on-time confirmations

How We Use Your Information

We use your information solely for the following purposes:

  • Sending SMS notifications about train delays, cancellations, and on-time status
  • Verifying your phone number through SMS verification codes
  • Managing your subscription preferences
  • Improving our service quality

Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored securely in our database with industry-standard security measures. We implement appropriate technical and organizational safeguards to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Twilio: For sending SMS notifications. Twilio's privacy policy applies to messages sent through their service.
  • NJ Transit API: For retrieving real-time train status information. We do not share your personal information with NJ Transit.

Data Sharing

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes. Your data is used exclusively for providing train delay notifications.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your stored data
  • Update your train preferences at any time
  • Unsubscribe from our service and request data deletion
  • Opt-out of specific notification types (delay alerts vs. on-time alerts)

Data Retention

We retain your subscription information for as long as your account is active. When you unsubscribe, your data is permanently deleted from our system within 30 days.

SMS Messaging

By subscribing, you consent to receive SMS messages from us regarding train delays and confirmations. Message and data rates may apply based on your mobile carrier's plan. You can unsubscribe at any time by texting STOP or using our unsubscribe feature on the website.

Children's Privacy

Our service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify active subscribers of any material changes via SMS or email. Continued use of the service after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
